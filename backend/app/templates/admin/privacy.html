Privacy — what leaves this box

Stays on this device, always: your password (hashed), announcements, boards and rules, usage counts, logs, and any API keys you add (encrypted).

Leaves your network, only to fetch your data:

Open-Meteo & National Weather Service your approximate coordinates — required to get forecasts and alerts
Open-Meteo geocoding (setup only) the ZIP code you type, once, to find coordinates

Never: no accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, no ads, no cloud copy of anything. If SignalShack-the-company disappeared tomorrow, this box would keep working exactly as it does today.
